Platform

Two tracks. One ambassador network.

A paid, two-track civic-engagement platform. Networks year-round on one track, turnout on Election Day on the other. Branded and configured per organization.

Track 1

Community Ambassador

Grow engaged networks between elections.

  • Ambassadors complete a short weekly civic challenge on health, wealth, or power.
  • Each ambassador shares via referral link or QR code with their own network.
  • For each challenge completed and each referral made, ambassadors earn a stipend.
  • Stipends are sized to cover a cell phone or internet bill -- civic engagement that pays for staying connected.

Track 2

Voting Ambassador

Activate networks for relational voter turnout.

  • Ambassadors graduate from the Community Ambassador track after training.
  • They upload their contacts; the platform identifies who is eligible to become a volunteer Vote Tripler.
  • A Vote Tripler commits to help three of their own contacts make a voting plan.
  • Ambassadors earn a small recruitment payment for each Vote Tripler they sign up.

Customizability

Your colors. Your domain. Your rules.

Hub3 is a platform, not a template. Every organization runs its own configuration -- what gets shown, who qualifies, how much an ambassador earns. Your program looks like yours, because it is.

Brand & domain

Custom colors, logos, and copy. Host on your own subdomain so the program reads as the org's, not Hub3's.

Content

Bring your own weekly challenges. Health, wealth, civic power -- or whatever curriculum your org is running.

Eligibility rules

Configure who qualifies as an ambassador or Vote Tripler. E.g. presidential-but-not-midterm voters in your county.

Communications

Customize the messages ambassadors send, the reminders they get, and the language used across the experience.

Stipend amounts

Set per-challenge pay, per-referral pay, and Vote Tripler recruitment payments to match your program budget.

Reporting

Track activity, payouts, and referrals at the ambassador, cohort, and program level -- exportable for funders.
