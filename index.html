What Hub3 does

Long-cycle infrastructure for community organizations.

Grow networks.

Ambassadors recruit their own communities and earn small stipends for civic work — week after week, year after year.

Engage between cycles.

Weekly civic challenges on health, wealth, and power keep networks active when no one else is paying attention.

Turn out the vote.

Voting Ambassadors recruit Vote Triplers, and field tools route every shift down to the door.

Product

The Ambassador platform.

A two-track platform that lets community organizations run their own paid civic-engagement programs — between elections and during them. Branding, content, eligibility rules, and stipend amounts are configured per organization.

  • Community Ambassadors grow engaged networks year-round through civic challenges and referrals.
  • Voting Ambassadors activate networks for relational turnout by recruiting Vote Triplers.
  • Fully customizable — your colors, your domain, your content, your eligibility rules.

Product · Field Targeting System

Turning the voter file into routes volunteers can actually walk.

A precinct-level data and dashboard platform for civic organizations and campaigns. Statewide voter records, voter history, polling places, address points, and precinct boundaries — joined into operational outputs your chairs and field staff actually use.

  • A county-and-precinct chair workflow, not a data scientist's notebook.
  • Household-level priority doors with plain-English turnout context.
  • Walkable route shifts capped at 90 minutes — zero exceed the cap in the current build.
  • Printable playbooks with audit and provenance, ready for a volunteer's hand.
