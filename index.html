What we share

Three things you can ask for.

Strategy docs.

The long-cycle organizing memos and county-level priority frameworks we use internally. Plain English, no jargon, written by people who actually do the work.

Maps.

Precinct and household maps for 11 NC counties -- the data layer that powers the Field Targeting System, packaged for one organizer to read and walk.

Contact sync.

We help you pull your contacts, voter-file matches, and friend-of-friend lookups into one workable list. Local data, no cloud upload, you keep everything.

Product · Field Targeting System

Turning the voter file into routes volunteers can actually walk.

A precinct-level data and dashboard platform for NC organizers and county chairs. Statewide voter records, voter history, polling places, address points, and precinct boundaries -- joined into operational outputs you can walk.

  • A county-and-precinct chair workflow, not a data scientist's notebook.
  • Household-level priority doors with plain-English turnout context.
  • Walkable route shifts capped at 90 minutes -- zero exceed the cap in the current build.
  • Printable playbooks with audit and provenance, ready for a volunteer's hand.
